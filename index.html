BENJI

Matcha, Music & Maltese Magic

Meet Benji, a white Maltese dog with a passion for matcha, an obsession for 2 Hollis, and an Italian heritage that fuels his soul.

Born in Italy, Benji brings old-world charm and fierce style to the modern world — always dressed like a Balenciaga runway star, yet forever humble with his boyfriend Logi by his side.

Benji’s Music Taste

Benji lives for the deep vibes of 2 Hollis and Sombur, mixing soulful beats with Italian passion. He appreciates classics like George Harrison, but also knows how to shade when needed.

For instance, Benji thinks Billy Idol mogs — pure rockstar energy. But when it comes to Niamh's Led Zeppelin obsession? Let’s just say Benji calls it terrible with an eye roll.

Matcha Obsession & Lifestyle

Benji’s mornings always start with a steaming cup of matcha — carefully brewed, frothy, and perfect. This ritual fuels his day filled with fashion, music, and lots of chicken.

Between chill moments with his boyfriend Logi and absorbing Balenciaga aesthetics, Benji balances old Italian tradition with new-age cool.
